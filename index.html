About Me

A developer with a passion for software creation. I have been more of a hobbyist in the world of programming for nearly ten years and in the last three years, have begun to pursue a formal education in various programming languages and paradigms.

I initially began honing my skills through graphic design works, primarily concerned with digital mediums. In doing so, I had become very knowledgeable and experienced using the Adobe suite of 2D raster and vector design products, namely Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I had also practiced 3D design in Maya and continue to hone those skills through Blender. After designing for a number of educational projects dealing with web design, I had begun to delve more into learning HTML5 and CSS in order to better understand the medium and taking a genuine interest in developing my programming skills beyond web front-end development.

I had become familiar with C# from self-teaching, practiced and educated in C, C++, and Java through college educational programs, and have learned the underlying principles of the OOP paradigm through both formal education and my own research. I have had some exposure to Python during my own studies and am hoping to expand more on that language in the future. However, the OOP paradigm is not my only exposure to programming languages. Indeed, I am familiar with and have done some progarmming in a few other language paradigms, namely Scheme/Racket and Prolog.

Whatever your own endeavors may seek to accomplish, I would love for the opportunity to participate in integrating computer technology and application software for your project, whether it be the entirety of your project's working pipeline or a small portion of it.
